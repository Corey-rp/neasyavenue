The story behind the lens

Hi, I'm Arnelle.

"The best moments aren't staged — they're discovered."

I'm Arnelle Dickens, a film and media student at Georgia State University finishing up my degree this December. But honestly? I've been a visual storyteller long before I had a title for it.

Neasy Avenue is where that vision lives — a space for portraits that feel like you, milestones worth remembering, and love stories that deserve to be preserved. I believe every person has a photo waiting to happen; I'm just the one with the camera.

I shoot with heart, I edit with intention, and I show up ready to make you feel comfortable from the first click to the last. Whether it's your graduation, your relationship, or just a season of life you want to hold onto — I've got you.

That photo? That's me in Amsterdam — proof that the best backdrops are the ones you stumble into.
